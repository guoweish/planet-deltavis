AI时代寻找大模型还没有学过的知识非常麻烦。因此我创建了一个叫deltavis的外星星球，并设定了一整套世界观，包含星球气候、生态系统等等。围绕deltavis的世界观设定，衍生出用于各类模型训练的语料库。可以使用qwen　max、qwen　image等模型作为教师模型，标注用于微调的文本和图片语料。
Finding knowledge that large models haven't been trained on is a real hassle in the AI era. That’s why I created an alien planet called Deltavis, complete with a fully fleshed-out worldview covering everything from its climate to its ecosystems. Building on the Deltavis lore, I’m putting together a corpus specifically for training various models. I plan to use models like Qwen Max and Qwen Image as "teacher models" to annotate both text and image data for fine-tuning.
